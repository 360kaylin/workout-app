# Holiday Fit Tracker

A static phone-friendly workout app for the 17 August holiday goal. It includes:

- A 5-6 day home workout plan using bodyweight and one 9 lb dumbbell.
- Weekly automatic progression from 7 July 2026 to 17 August 2026.
- A full workout interval timer.
- Exercise form details for every move.
- Water, food, protein, workout completion, weight, and waist tracking.
- A post-holiday four-week repeat cycle.

## Add It To Your Phone

- iPhone Safari: open the site, tap Share, then `Add to Home Screen`.
- Android Chrome: open the site, tap the menu, then `Add to Home screen` or `Install app`.
- site link, https://360kaylin.github.io/workout-app/

## Notes

- Tracker data is stored locally in the browser on the device you use.
- Clearing site data or using another phone/browser will not carry the tracker history across.
- The plan assumes you are healthy enough for intense exercise. Stop if you get sharp pain, dizziness, chest pain, or unusual symptoms.
- Fat loss cannot be targeted only from the stomach. The plan combines full-body training, core work, protein, water, and a small calorie deficit to reduce overall body fat while building muscle.

## Nutrition Targets

- Starting weight: `63.8 kg`.
- Protein target: about `102 g/day`, with a practical range of `90-115 g/day`.
- Water target: `2500 ml/day`, with more if you sweat a lot.
- Focus foods: lean protein, eggs, fish, Greek yoghurt, tofu, beans, lentils, potatoes, rice, oats, wholegrain bread, fruit, and vegetables.
- Reduce: sugary drinks, alcohol, big snack portions, and takeaway extras.
